# PushupPro Landing Page - Project Requirements

## Core Objective
Build a single-purpose, premium marketing landing page for the PushupPro workout app. The singular goal is to drive app installations (iOS and Android). 

## Target Action
- Users must be presented with two primary, equal-weight CTAs: "Download on the App Store" and "Get it on Google Play".
- These buttons must be official Apple/Google branded assets, not custom pill buttons.
- CTAs must appear in the Hero section AND the Closing CTA section.

## Design Philosophy (Inspired by eco.com)
- **Quality over quantity:** Fewer sections (max 6-7), massive vertical breathing room (`8rem` section padding on desktop).
- **One clear idea per section:** Do not repeat layout patterns (e.g., do not use a 3-card grid twice).
- **Motion as a material:** Every transition and scroll animation must share the same easing curve to feel like a single, cohesive material. 

## Excluded Features (To keep the scope narrow)
- **No Newsletter Signup:** Do not implement the email capture form seen in the eco footer. 
- **No Pricing Tables:** The app is free-to-install with a subscription inside the app itself. No pricing on the web.
- **No Dashboards or Docs:** This is a marketing site only. No user login, no API documentation.
- **Avoid Framer Motion:** The animation stack is strictly GSAP. Do not use Framer Motion.

## Tone & Audience
- Tone: High-end, energetic but controlled, athletic, trustworthy.
- Visuals: Dark cinematic hero, off-white clean backgrounds.
